Electrical Engineering for Minetest.

This mods implements electrical engineering in Minetest, making possible to build electrical grids and circuits that behave in a similar fashion of circuits in real life.

Basic components

The most basic components that we will be trying to implement first are:

  • Wires
  • Light Bulb
  • Switch
  • Battery
  • Common Ground
